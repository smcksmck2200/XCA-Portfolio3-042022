I immediately visited the local Employment Services Office. I used counselors as advisors to pilot me in the right direction toward gainful employment opportunities thus leading me to enroll in Tech Louisville, a training program hosted by KentuckianaWorks. I completed the Google IT Support Professional Certificate in 4 months. I began working at Compucom as an IT Support Desk Call Center Agent. I continued to learn. I resigned from Compucom to pursue a Data Analytics Immersive Fellowship course through General Assembly with over 400hrs of dedicated learning hours. Here, we learned about and completed tasks using Excel, Sql, Python, Tableau, PostgresSql, Tableau Prep. We were also introduced to Machine Learning, Power BI and a host of other concepts involving Data Analytics. We were assigned homework, bi- weekly graded projects, as well as a final capstone project. Now I feel I am ready for the next step in my quest to better my lifestyle through education and work experience.
